Tombola 2.0 Update

Version 2.0 is now available with dozens of new features and compatibility with the latest OSs

Tombola 2.0 is an all-new version of the application, available starting today on the App Store for iPadOS and macOS.

Here's a sneak peek at what's new:

  • Full compatibility with iPadOS 14, macOS 11 Big Sur and the new Macs with Apple Silicon
  • The app is now entirely localized in English
  • The ability to view recent games in the Welcome page, and to generate a ticket to play on device without leaving the app
  • The game link now contains a meeting room on Skype Meet Now or Jitsi Meet together with the ticket
  • iCloud support allows you to store and sync your games across all your devices
  • Text-To-Speech functionality is more discrete and works with more languages

Just as the previous version, Tombola 2.0 is completely free
