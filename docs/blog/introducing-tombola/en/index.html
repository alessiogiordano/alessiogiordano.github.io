Introducing Tombola

A new iPadOS and macOS app for playing a game of Tombola via any video call service

Today I release Tombola on the iPad and Mac App Stores.

This application enables you to play a game of Tombola via screen sharing on Skype, Zoom and others. Just install the app and create a new game: via the "Invite" button you can share a url with other players that will distribute to them randomly-generated cards; this means you can play with as many people as you want.

The app is completely free and it is available now. Download now
